What makes Hitch different

Unlike a taxi service where you pay an expensive fee each ride, on hitch you pay a monthly subscription to get access to recomendations of drivers on our app who on their daily commute to college, bring others along with.

Social

Meet new people in your area who have the same commute as you, what a great way to make new friends!

Cost

This service costs only 15euro a month! In comparison to the cheapest alternative to daily transport, Dublin bus that is 267% more expensive than us. In addition the money from students stays with students, from drivers, to riders, to us.

Convience

No need to walk to bus stops, change buses or trains, just hop in, join in and go on.
